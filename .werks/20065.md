# Tactical overview snapin: large numbers ran out of their box

key        | value
---------- | ---
date       | 2026-07-20T07:52:50.546121+00:00
version    | 3.0.0b1
class      | fix
edition    | community
component  | multisite
level      | 1
compatible | yes

In the sidebar's "Overview" (tactical overview) snapin, very large host and
service counts – for example on installations with millions of services – ran
out of their rounded boxes and overlapped the neighbouring columns.

The numbers now stay inside their box. Should a value still be too long for the
available width, it is cut off with an ellipsis instead of overflowing, and the
full value is shown as a tooltip when you hover over it.
